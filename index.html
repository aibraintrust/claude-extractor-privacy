Claude & ChatGPT Chat Extractors – Privacy Policy

These Chrome extensions do not collect, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All processing of conversations happens locally within your browser. Their sole function is to allow you to export your Claude.ai or ChatGPT chats as JSON files. No information is sent to external servers or third parties.

Scope

  • Claude Chat Extractor – Chrome Web Store link
  • ChatGPT Chat Extractor – pending publication

Both follow the same principles: local-only processing, no external transmission, no data collection.
